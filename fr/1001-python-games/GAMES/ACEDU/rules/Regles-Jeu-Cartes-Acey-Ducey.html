ACEYDU ACEY DUCEY CARD GAME Description This is a simulation of the Acey Ducey card game. In the game, the dealer (the computer) deals two cards face up. You have the option to bet or not bet depending on whether or not you think the next card dealt will have a value between the first two. Your initial money (Q) is set at $100; you can change instruction 170 if you want to start with more or less than $100. The game continues until you lose all your money or you interrupt the program.
