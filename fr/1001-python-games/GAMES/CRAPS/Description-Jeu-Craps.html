CRAPS : ================= Difficulty: 7 ----------------- The game "Craps" is a dice game of chance in which the player bets on the outcome of rolling two dice. In the first round, the player wins if the sum of the dice is 7 or 11, and loses if the sum is 2, 3, or 12. If the sum of the dice is 4, 5, 6, 8, 9, or 10, this becomes the player's "point", and they continue to roll the dice until that number is rolled again (in which case the player wins) or a 7 is rolled (in which case the player loses).

Game rules: 1. At the start of the game, the player rolls two dice. 2. If the sum of the dice is 7 or 11, the player wins. 3. If the sum of the dice is 2, 3, or 12, the player loses. 4. If the sum of the dice is 4, 5, 6, 8, 9, or 10, this number becomes the "point". 5. After establishing the "point", the player continues to roll the dice until: - the "point" is rolled again, then the player wins. - a 7 is rolled, then the player loses. ----------------- Algorithm: 1. Generate random values for two dice (from 1 to 6). 2. Calculate the sum of the obtained values. 3. If the sum is 7 or 11, display a win message and go to step 7. 4. If the sum is 2, 3, or 12, display a lose message and go to step 7. 5. Save the sum as "point". 6. Start a loop: 6.1 Generate random values for two dice. 6.2 Calculate the sum of the obtained values. 6.3 If the sum is equal to the "point", display a win message and go to step 7. 6.4 If the sum is 7, display a lose message and go to step 7. 6.5 Otherwise, repeat the loop from step 6. 7. End of game. ----------------- Flowchart:
