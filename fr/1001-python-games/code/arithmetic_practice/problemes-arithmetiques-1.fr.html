Easy Level

  1. Grandma has 15 apples, and she wants to share them equally among 5 grandchildren. How many apples will each grandchild receive?
  2. To prepare 2 pies, 6 eggs are needed. How many eggs will be needed for 3 pies?
  3. The height of each stair step is 10 cm. To what height will a person climb after going up 3 steps?
  4. 12 liters of water must be poured into bottles of 3 liters each. How many bottles will be needed?
  5. 3 kg of oranges cost 180 rubles. How much will 2 kg of the same oranges cost?
  6. 28 chairs must be arranged in 4 rows equally. How many chairs will there be in each row?
  7. To prepare 5 cups of tea, 2 tea bags are needed. How many tea bags are needed for 10 cups of tea?
  8. A person took 3 steps the first minute, and 1 more step each subsequent minute. How many steps did they take in the fifth minute?
  9. 24 candies must be shared equally among 6 friends. How many candies will each friend receive?
  10. A car traveled 200 km in 4 hours. How many kilometers will it travel in 2 hours at the same speed?

Medium Level

  1. 55 pies must be shared among 8 children. How many pies will each child receive, and how many pies will be left?
  2. On a map with a scale of 1:2500, the distance between two houses is 4.8 cm. What is the real distance between these houses (in meters)?
  3. Find the sum of the first 7 terms of the arithmetic progression: 2, 5, 8, 11...
  4. 150 liters of milk must be poured into bottles of 1.5 liters each. How many bottles will be needed?
  5. An alloy contains 5 parts gold and 2 parts silver. How many grams of silver are contained in 350 grams of the alloy?
  6. In a movie theater, the first row has 25 seats, and each subsequent row has 3 more seats. How many seats are there in total in the first 9 rows?
  7. 76 kg of potatoes must be packed into bags of 5 kg each. How many full bags will be obtained and how many kg will be left?
  8. To make a cake for 8 people, 4 eggs are needed. How many eggs will be needed for a cake for 10 people?
  9. The trail starts at the 5-meter mark and increases by 7 meters every hour. What will be the length of the trail after 5 hours?
  10. 20. 285 books must be distributed equally on 15 shelves. How many books will there be on each shelf?
  11. To make cookies, 200 grams of sugar and 400 grams of flour are needed. If you only have 150 grams of sugar, how much flour do you need?
  12. In the training room, the first row has 12 seats, and each subsequent row has 2 more seats. How many seats are there in the 10th row?
  13. 184 kg of candies must be packed into bags of 2 kg each. How many bags will be obtained and how many kg will be left?
  14. On a map with a scale of 1:10000, the distance between cities is 12 cm. What is the distance between these cities in real life?
  15. Find the sum of the first 8 terms of the arithmetic progression: 1, 6, 11, 16...
  16. To make 3 suits, 12 meters of fabric are needed. How many meters of fabric will be needed for 7 suits?
  17. At the mill, 432 kg of grain are ground in 8 hours. How many kilograms of grain will be ground in 12 hours at the same working speed?
  18. The family has a garden with 60 trees, each producing 25 kg of fruit per year. How many kilograms of fruit will they harvest in 3 years?
  19. At the farm, there are 48 chickens, each laying 5 eggs per week. How many eggs will they lay in 2 weeks?

Difficult Level

  1. A company with a profit of 5,789,000 rubles must pay a tax of 15%, and the rest must be divided among 25 shareholders, 10% of which are for company development. How many rubles will each shareholder receive?
  2. On a map with a scale of 1:1500, the distance between two objects is 11.4 cm. What will be the distance between these objects on a map with a scale of 1:750?
  3. A person took out a loan for 15 months, with a payment increasing by 30 rubles each month. The first payment is 400 rubles. What is the total amount of loan payments?
  4. To paint a wall with an area of 75 square meters, 18 liters of paint are needed. How many liters of paint will be needed to paint a wall with an area of 150 square meters?
  5. The factory produced 7800 parts, 12% of which were defective. The remaining parts must be packed into boxes of 40 pieces each. How many full boxes will be obtained?
  6. To get 500 g of solution, 25 g of salt must be used. How much solution can be obtained if you have 225 grams of salt?
  7. In the theater, there are 20 rows, the first row has 15 seats, and each subsequent row has 2 more seats. How many seats are there in total in the theater?
  8. To tile a 7x5 meter wall, 350 tiles are needed. How many tiles are needed to tile a 12x6 meter wall?
  9. 20 builders can complete a job in 30 days. How many builders need to be hired to complete the same job in 20 days?
  10. An employee's salary increases by 15000 rubles each year. If in the first year, they received 50000 rubles per month, what will be their total income over 7 years, considering that each month has 30 working days?
  11. 1250 meters of wire must be cut into pieces of 12 meters. How many pieces will be obtained and how many meters will remain?
  12. To prepare syrup, 2 parts water and 3 parts sugar are needed. How much sugar is needed to prepare 850 ml of syrup?
  13. A runner ran 25 km the first week, and each subsequent week, they increased the distance by 5 km. How many km did they run in 7 weeks?
  14. The warehouse has 2545 units of goods that need to be packed into boxes. Each box contains 28 items. How many full boxes will be needed?
  15. The tank contains 560 liters of water. If 15% of the water evaporated in one day, how many liters of water will remain after 3 days at the same evaporation rate?
  16. The factory produces 2500 parts per day. 15% are sent for export, and the rest is divided equally among 10 partners. How many parts will each partner receive?
  17. To build a 12 km long road, a team uses 20 machines, each working 6 hours a day. How many machines will be needed to build the same road in half the time, if each works 8 hours a day?
  18. Find the sum of the first 12 terms of the arithmetic progression: -3, 1, 5, 9...
  19. 342 students must be divided into groups of 18 people. How many groups will be obtained and how many students will remain?
  20. On a map with a scale of 1:25000, the distance between two points is 16 cm. What is the distance on a map with a scale of 1:8000?
  21. A recipe for 6 servings requires 300 grams of flour, 150 grams of sugar, and 75 grams of oil. If 11 servings are to be prepared, how much of each ingredient will be needed?
